How to get the best results

  • ☀️
    Good Lighting
    Ensure the leaf is well-lit, preferably with natural daylight. Avoid harsh shadows.
  • 🔍
    Focus is Key
    Make sure the camera is focused sharply on the spots, discoloration, or damage.
  • 🍃
    Isolate the Leaf
    Try to have only one leaf in the frame. A plain background (like a piece of white paper) helps the AI massively.
